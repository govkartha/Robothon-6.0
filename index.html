Robothon 6.0

Unleash the power of Golems!!! Here comes Robothon V6!! Continue glavanizing the next generation of innovators with your bots. At the dawn of 2020, we are unveiling new roads to the plethora of opportunities and innovations in the world of artificial intelligence towards the greater good of our species!! Be recognized at the technical extravaganza of IEEE SB CEC. And, you may invent the next super amazing thing that changes our world! Want a chance at this awesome opportunity?

The challenge of this hackathon is that each participating team(consisting of 1 to 4 members) must design, fabricate and program a robot within a 24 hour time period. The main goal of Robothon 6.0 is to offer a platform for robotic enthusiasts to showcase their talent and acquire new skills. Furthermore, we also believe that the intriguing backdrop of a hackathon will give birth to a few prompt and ingenious ideas.
